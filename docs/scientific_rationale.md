# Scientific Rationale

RewireScope is built around the idea that ecological systems are changing dependency architectures. Composition tells us what is present. Topology tells us what depends on what. Timing tells us when dependencies execute. Environmental forcing tells us what loads or relaxes the system. Rewiring tells us how the architecture moves.

The scientific risk is overclaiming. The implementation therefore treats evidence provenance as a first-class object. Observed pairwise interactions can support direct rewiring visualization. Historical reconstructions can support bounded contrast. Abundance and environmental time series can support inferred dynamic dependency, but not observed rewiring.

The proof of concept is designed to make that distinction visible while still showing why static state metrics miss early movement in the operating architecture of ecosystems.
